ABOUT

Vellore Institute of Technology (VIT) has always been at the forefront and achieved several national and international accolades. The students at VIT have been provided with ample opportunities to unleash their genius. The annual techno-management knowledge carnival of VIT, “graVITas” attracts students from India and Abroad and provides a platform to display their spectacular talents and innovations. The 11th Edition of graVITas is scheduled during October 11-13, 2019.

This year graVITas is going to hone the technological skills of the students and researchers with innovative events in every avenue of engineering coupled with MEGATHON (Non-stop prototype/code/idea making competition), Workshops, Seminars and an International Conference with specific theme. This year extravaganza goes with a theme “Technology Driven Transformation”. The primary objective of this year carnival is to address the feasibility of providing solutions which can simplify the lives of common people with the use of NANO in engineering, science and technology.
